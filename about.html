My Career Story

I am an entertainment executive with successful experience in content strategy, acquisitions and media planning. I build targeted program libraries for consumer-facing brands, working with studios and distribution partners to license movies and television series that attract and retain customers.

I have helped grow audiences for networks like Comedy Central and BBC America, and I have led content strategies behind rebrands at TV Land and Paramount Network (formerly Spike TV). I’m comfortable working with teams from Product and Creative to Finance and Ad Sales. And I’m someone who understands that content strategy and audience acquisition go hand-in-hand with research insights and data analytics.

Looking to expand beyond traditional media, I have recently completed a Bootcamp in Data Analytics at Columbia University’s School of Engineering to become more fluent in the latest computer languages and business intelligence tools. I'm now able to wrangle big datasets myself to derive insights, and I can illustrate those insights with front-end visualizations. These skills are a valuable complement to my experience in content strategy and acquisitions.

Whether working in an analytical role where I'd be hands-on with the data, or working in a content role collaborating with a data team, I am most at home in that space where art and science intersect in media.
